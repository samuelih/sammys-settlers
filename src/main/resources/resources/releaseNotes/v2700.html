2.7.00

  • Gameplay:
    • Player can cancel Monopoly, Year of Plenty cards while choosing resources, or Knight card while moving the robber or pirate
      • Dev card is returned to their hand
      • That player's client must be v2.7.00 or newer
    • New optional house rule: Allow undo building and moving pieces (client v2.7.00 or newer)
      • Is on by default when client and server both support the option
      • Builds/moves which reveal fog hexes can't be undone, so players can't peek to choose the best hex
  • I18N:
    • Added German translation (thank you Eudoxia and Quasigroup)
    • Updated Polish translation (thank you KotCzarny)
  • Client:
    • Game window:
      • For visibility when a player builds, highlight most recently placed piece until end of their turn
      • Bank/port trade: Bugfix: After making a trade, if player wants to undo that trade but accidentally clicks "Bank/Port" instead of "Undo", couldn't then click Undo
      • Road Building: Confirm when player skips (cancels) placing second free road or ship
      • Statistics dialog shows player's resource rolls and trades
    • Main window:
      • Version info is underlined; click to show new "About Sammys-Settlers" dialog
      • "What's New" info can be shown from About dialog
  • Server:
    • Bugfix: If player cancels Road Building with Cancel Ship button, server now allows playing another card
    • Send a message to idle clients every few minutes, so their ISP or NAT doesn't disconnect them from games
    • New *MUTE* and *UNMUTE* commands for game's creator or admin to manage that game's chat with players and observers
  • Other changes: see Versions.md
